Elkhart County ClubhouseRecord Retention Requirements

Financial records:
	Bank Records (statements, reconciliations, registers, slips) - 3 years
	Payroll records - 7 years
	Taxes, Audit and year-end reports - keep permanently
	Vendor contracts, invoices and accounts payable - 7 years
	Contribution records - 7 years
	Grant records - 7 years
	Depreciation schedules - keep permanently

Legal correspondence, deeds, mortgages - keep permanently
Board minutes, bylaws and policies - keep permanently
Insurance records, accident reports, claims and policies - keep permanently
Member and staff files - keep permanently
Correspondence and employment applications - 3 years
